Dataset Description

Originally, the dataset was from Kaggle, with 9994 rows and 21 columns. The dataset is about Supermarket performance from January 2014 until December 2017. In the process of analysis, we breakdown and remove the outlier.

      Insight

      • The highest sales were in November 2017 at 48.520
      • The sales trend is relatively the same almost every year, namely that in Q4 it increased while in Q1 it decreased.

      Recommendation

      Increased marketing strategies or promotions for products in Office Supplies and Technology have generated high sales.

      Insight

      • Product’s that have discounts tend to affect the level of sales trend every month.

      Recommendation

      Make a bundling package, where the best-selling product is bundled with the least popular product.

      Insight

      • The segmentation with the highest purchase transactions are Consumer, followed by the Corporate and Home Officer segments.

      Recommendation

      Give rewards for segmentation that have frequent transactions or create a membership to increase loyalty.

      Insight

      • Standard class as a preferable shipping mode from customer crossover all the region.

      Recommendation

      Give a free shipping fee and special handling for ship mode except Standard Class to attract the curiosity of customers to try it.

      Insight

      • The West is the region that has the highest sales and total orders.
      • Of all the states in the United States, California is the state with the highest sales and total orders.
      • It can be said that the West is the most profitable state.

      Recommendation

      Maximize sales in states or regions based on accumulated sales and total orders like in Michigan and Virginia with the most potential.

      Insight

      • Technology and Furniture are the 2 best selling categories based on Total Sales.
      • Office Supplies is the most items have been sold.
      • Products in the Technology and Office Supplies category can be the main focus for marketing strategies.

      Recommendation

      Providing stock for frequently sold products, such as office supply in the store.
